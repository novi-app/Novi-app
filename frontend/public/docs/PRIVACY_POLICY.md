# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

**Novi** | Effective Date: March 2026 | Beta Version

## Privacy Policy

At Novi, your privacy isn't an afterthought — it's part of how we build. This policy explains what
we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.
***

### Your Privacy Matters to Us

Novi is an AI travel companion designed to help solo travelers move through decisions faster
and explore with more confidence. To do that well, we need to understand how you interact with
the app. We take that responsibility seriously. We collect only what we need, we don't sell your
data, and we're transparent about everything we do with it.
***

### Information We Collect

**Information you provide directly**
- First name (collected at signup)
- Travel preferences, including dietary restrictions, activity interests, budget range, and
travel intent (such as eating, exploring, or connecting)

**Information collected automatically**
- Location data, used to surface relevant recommendations near you
- Behavioral interaction data, including scroll patterns, tap activity, time spent on screens,
and revisit behavior — this is how Novi detects when you're experiencing decision
paralysis and steps in to help
- Device type and operating system

**What we do not collect**
- Payment information
- Passport or identification details
- Any data from third-party apps or services
***

### How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Deliver personalized recommendations based on your location, intent, and preferences
- Detect moments of decision paralysis and surface timely suggestions
- Improve Novi's recommendation engine and freeze detection accuracy
- Communicate with you about your beta experience, including feedback requests and
product updates
- Maintain the security and performance of the app
During this beta period, your interaction data also helps us understand how Novi is performing
so we can improve it before wider launch.
***

### Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from
unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your data is stored securely and access is limited to the
Novi core team only. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we are
committed to protecting your information and will notify you promptly if a breach occurs that
affects your data.
***

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Request correction** of any inaccurate information
- **Request deletion** of your data at any time
- **Opt out** of future communications from us
- **Withdraw consent** to data collection, which will result in discontinuation of your beta
access
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [noviteam.ai@gmail.com](mailto:noviteam.ai@gmail.com) and we'll respond within 5
business days.
